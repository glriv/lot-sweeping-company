Our Story

Founded in 2013, Lot Sweeping Company has been providing exceptional parking lot sweeping and maintenance services to businesses throughout the region. What started as a small operation with just one truck has grown into a full-service sweeping company with a fleet of modern equipment and a dedicated team of professionals.

We understand that a clean parking lot is the first impression your customers have of your business. That's why we're committed to delivering thorough, reliable service that keeps your property looking its best year-round.

Our team takes pride in using environmentally responsible practices and state-of-the-art equipment to ensure the highest quality results. We work with property managers, shopping centers, restaurants, office buildings, and construction sites to provide customized sweeping solutions that fit their unique needs.
